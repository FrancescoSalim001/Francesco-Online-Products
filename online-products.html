What is a password generator?

A password generator is a tool or software that creates random, complex passwords for users. These passwords are designed to be difficult to guess or crack, enhancing security for online accounts and sensitive information.


What is a gmail generator?

A Gmail generator typically refers to a tool or service that creates temporary or disposable Gmail accounts. These generators allow users to create email addresses without needing to go through the standard sign-up process, often for purposes like:

1. Privacy: Users can avoid giving out their primary email addresses when signing up for websites or services.
2. Spam Prevention: Temporary emails can help reduce spam in a user's main inbox.
3. Testing: Developers may use generated accounts to test applications or services.
